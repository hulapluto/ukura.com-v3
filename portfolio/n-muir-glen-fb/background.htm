Chef Will Gilson

• At age 17, Gilson was the chef at his
father's farm restaurant, Herb Lyceum.

• From 2006 to 2010, Chef Gilson
was the executive chef and proprietor
of Boston's Garden at The Cellar.

• His newest venture is EAT, a pop-up
restaurant he operates with business
partner Aaron Cohen.

• Tomato Philosophy •

Chef Gilson's favorite use of canned tomatoes is for "hearty, slow cooked dishes, like soups, pasta sauces, and even jam to serve with cured meats."

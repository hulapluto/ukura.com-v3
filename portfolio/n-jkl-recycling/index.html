Current News

 

Commercial business’ that reside in the MN, Brainerd Lakes Area Can Request a Simple e-waste plan from JKL Recycling in which the commercial business can receive a free Commercial E-waste pickup for their business free of charge. In this you can lower your e-waste stream while saving money and time.

 

Free JKL Recycling recyclable items:

  • COMPUTERS
  • LAPTOPS
  • CELL PHONES
  • SERVERS
  • Switches
  • CABLES
  • Tablets
  • LCD Displays
  • Mp3players
  • Gaming devices
  • Printers
  • Scanners
  • Fax machine
  • Routers wired or wireless
  • Telecom equipment of any kind
  • Any types of lithium, cadmium, NiMH or rechargeable batteries (excluding alkaline)
